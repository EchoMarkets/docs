Echo Markets is a prediction market that runs directly on Bitcoin.
People can create and settle prediction markets, buy and sell “yes” or “no” positions, and collect winnings or fees — all directly on Bitcoin itself, without using sidechains, middlemen, or needing to trust anyone else. Just using Charms Protocol.{% .lead %}
